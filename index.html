    PARENT STOCK

    We rear the Arbor Acres Parent Stock breed from Aviagen, which is the world's leading poultry breeding company known for its pedigree line of broiler chickens and turkeys. Arbor Acres has been in business for more than 75 years and is one of the oldest and most respected names in the poultry industry.

    The Aviagen Group is the global market leader in poultry genetics. Aviagen has three poultry breeding brands: Arbor Acres, Ross and Indian River, delivering day-old grandparent and parent stock chicks worldwide. These brands are among the most recognized and respected names in the industry and each has a proven record of success in addition to a large and loyal global customer base.

    Our Company uses the Arbor Acres chicken breed to produce hatching eggs and broiler day old chicks. The Arbo Acres breed is the world's most successful combination of male and female lines with excellent feed conversion and meat yield combined with highly reproductive females, producing healthy day old broiler chicks.

    The Arbor Acres breeder produces a high number of eggs combined with good hatchability to optimize chick cost in situations where broiler performance is important.

    HATCHERY MANAGEMENT

    HPF has successfully reared and hatched quality day old chicks from Kienyeji and Exotic which are sold to both our out-grower farmers and agents (distributors) in various parts of the country.

    HPF has two hatcheries located in Kagio and Kerugoya.

    In integrated operations, HPF satisfies the demands of customers who require a bird which performs consistently well and has the versatility to meet a broad range of end product requirements.
