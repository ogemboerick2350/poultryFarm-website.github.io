Slaughtering Department

Our slaughter house is government certified and has a slaughtering capacity is 2,000 chicken per day. Our chicken processing line is inspected by meat inspectors before delivery to our clients.

The slaughter capacity can be increased to 5,000 chicken per day as we expand. The chicken processing line is inspected by health officials to ensure that the products are of good quality and are fit for human consumption.



Below are some of the procedures we follow in preparing our products.
